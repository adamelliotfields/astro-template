---
title: Embedding YouTube Videos
description: A small MDX demo for embedding YouTube videos with an Astro component.
pubDate: 2026-01-12
---

import YouTube from '@/components/YouTube.astro'

The `YouTube` component accepts a video ID or YouTube URL and normalizes them to a privacy-enhanced `youtube-nocookie.com` embed inside an `astro-youtube` custom element with a shadow root. The thumbnail image, if available, is displayed while the video is loading:

```jsx
<YouTube id="mgkwZqVkrwo" />
```

<YouTube id="mgkwZqVkrwo" />

You can also start at an offset in seconds:

```jsx
<YouTube id="mgkwZqVkrwo" start={155} />
```

<YouTube id="mgkwZqVkrwo" start={155} />

Here's what a portrait aspect Short looks like:

```jsx
<YouTube src="https://youtube.com/shorts/aXcuz6fn8_w" />
```
