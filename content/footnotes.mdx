Footnotes are useful when the main text should stay readable, but a small aside still belongs with the article.[^first]

Astro will append a "Footnotes" `h2` heading to the bottom of the post with `id="footnote-label"`.

[^first]: This is the first footnote. It is referenced twice in the post body.
